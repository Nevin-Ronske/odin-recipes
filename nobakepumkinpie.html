No Bake Pumpkin Pie Recipe

No Bake Pumpkin Pie

Ingredients

  • Graham cracker crust
  • Unflavored gelatin
  • Cinnamon
  • Ginger
  • Nutmeg
  • Salt
  • Sweetened condensed milk
  • Eggs
  • Pumpkin puree
Steps
  1. Combine the gelatin, spices, and salt in a saucepan.
  2. Stir in one can of the sweetened condensed milk and 2 eggs.
  3. Cook over low heat until thick.
  4. Stir in the pumpkin, then pour into the graham cracker crust.
  5. Chill until set.
